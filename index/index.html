PVPSIT

Prasad V. Potluri Siddhartha Institute of Technology (PVPSIT) is a prestigious engineering college located in Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh. Established in 1998, it is affiliated with Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University (JNTU) and accredited by NAAC with an 'A+' grade and NBA for its academic excellence. PVPSIT offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various engineering disciplines, emphasizing quality education, research, and innovation. The college provides state-of-the-art infrastructure, well-equipped labs, and experienced faculty to foster holistic student development. It is renowned for its student-centered approach, industry collaborations, and placement opportunities in leading companies.

Services Offered


Hostel Facility For Girls

The hostel facility for girls offers a safe, secure, and comfortable environment with 24/7 security and CCTV surveillance. Rooms are well-furnished and designed for a pleasant stay, ensuring proper ventilation and hygiene.

Mentoring System in PVPSIT

he mentoring system at PVPSIT is designed to provide personalized guidance and support to students. Each mentor is assigned a group of students to monitor their academic progress, personal development, and career goals.

100% Ragging Free Campus

PVPSIT ensures a 100% ragging-free campus, maintaining a safe and welcoming environment for all students. Strict anti-ragging policies are in place, with zero tolerance for any form of harassment or misconduct.

Extension Activities

PVPSIT actively engages students in extension activities to promote social responsibility and holistic development. These include community service programs, awareness drives, environmental campaigns, and collaborations with NGOs

Women Cell in PVPSIT

The Women Cell at PVPSIT is dedicated to empowering female students and staff by ensuring a safe, inclusive, and supportive campus environment. It organizes workshops, seminars, and awareness programs on women's rights, self-defense, and career development.

Grievance Redressal

The Grievance Redressal system at PVPSIT ensures that students and staff can voice their concerns in a fair and transparent manner. A dedicated committee addresses issues related to academics, campus facilities, and personal well-being promptly.
